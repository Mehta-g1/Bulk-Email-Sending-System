Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to the Email Sender documentation. This guide provides all the information you need to use our bulk email sending system effectively. Whether you're a developer integrating our system or a user managing campaigns, this documentation will walk you through the features and functionalities.

Getting Started

1. Sign Up and Log In

To begin, you need to create an account. Navigate to the Sign Up page and fill in your details. Once registered, you can log in using your credentials on the Login page.

2. App-Specific Passwords

To send emails, you need to use an "App Password" from your email provider (e.g., Gmail, Outlook). This is a 16-digit code that gives a third-party app or device permission to access your email account. It's more secure than using your main password.

For instructions on how to generate an app password for your email provider, please refer to their official documentation:

  • Google/Gmail
  • Microsoft/Outlook

During sign-up, you will be asked for an "Email Password". This is where you should enter your app-specific password.

3. Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you will be redirected to your dashboard. The dashboard provides a central hub for all your email-related activities, including sending emails, managing recipients, and viewing your profile.

How to Use the System

Managing Recipients

You can add, edit, and delete recipients from your contact list. This allows you to maintain a clean and up-to-date list of contacts for your email campaigns.

  • To add a new recipient, click the "Add Recipient" button on your dashboard.
  • To edit or delete a recipient, find them in the recipient list on your dashboard and use the corresponding buttons.

Managing Email Templates

You can create, edit, and delete email templates to reuse for your campaigns.

  • Navigate to the Templates page to view your existing templates.
  • From there, you can create a new template, edit an existing one, or delete a template.
  • You can also set a "Primary" template, which will be the default template used when sending emails.

Sending Emails

From the dashboard, you can easily send emails to a list of recipients.

  1. Select the recipients you want to email from the list on your dashboard.
  2. Click the "Send Mail" button.
  3. The system will use your primary email template to send the emails to the selected recipients.
